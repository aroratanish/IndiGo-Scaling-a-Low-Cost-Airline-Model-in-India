# ✈️ IndiGo Case Study

### Scaling a Low-Cost Airline Model in India

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive case study of IndiGo, India’s leading airline, focusing on how it successfully implemented a low-cost carrier (LCC) model to achieve market dominance.

The study analyzes IndiGo’s **business model, growth strategy, operational efficiency, and unit economics**, along with its competitive positioning in the Indian aviation industry.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* To understand how IndiGo scaled using a low-cost strategy
* To analyze key drivers of profitability in the aviation sector
* To evaluate operational and strategic decisions behind its success

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts Covered

* Low-Cost Carrier (LCC) Business Model
* Cost Leadership Strategy
* Unit Economics in Aviation
* Fleet Standardization
* Competitive Analysis
* SWOT Analysis

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Efficiency and cost control are critical in price-sensitive markets
* Fleet standardization reduces operational complexity
* High aircraft utilization directly improves profitability
* Strong execution can outperform innovation in competitive industries
